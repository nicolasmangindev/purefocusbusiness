Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and the data associated with the PureFocus application, you can request this by writing to us at purfocus.contact@gmail.com.

Upon deletion

  • Your account information (email, identifiers) will be deleted from our systems.
  • Data related to in-app subscriptions (via Apple/Google) will remain managed by these platforms in accordance with their policies.
  • Legal billing data will be retained for the mandatory legal period (10 years).

Your request will be processed within 30 days maximum.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to purfocus.contact@gmail.com
  2. Include in the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Specify your user identifier or the email address associated with your account
  4. We will confirm the deletion by email within 30 days

Important information

Consequences of deletion:

  • Deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • You will lose access to all your data (tasks, sessions, statistics)
  • Your subscription will be terminated (no refund for the current period)
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use PureFocus in the future

Alternative: If you simply want to cancel your subscription without deleting your account, consult our Terms of Service page for cancellation instructions.

Your GDPR rights

Account deletion is part of your rights under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation). To learn more about your other rights (access, rectification, portability, etc.), consult our Privacy Policy.
